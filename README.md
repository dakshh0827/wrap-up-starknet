## The Problem

The internet is drowning in content. Readers can't trust what they find, curators have no provenance, and there's no verifiable record that an article ever mattered to anyone. Traditional platforms gatekeep what gets seen вАФ with zero transparency.

## Our Solution

**Wrap-Up** lets anyone paste a URL, get an AI-generated deep analysis, and mint that curation permanently on Starknet. Every article, research report, and comparison lives on-chain вАФ with community votes, comments, and curator reputation all verifiably recorded. No middlemen. No black boxes.

### рЯ§Ц AI Research Engine
